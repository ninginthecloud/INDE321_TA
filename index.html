Welcome to INDE 321 TA session

TA session will mainly focus on discussing R and homework problems. Here you can find all the materials (slides and codes) used for IND E 321 TA session in spring 2016 at Industrial Engineering department at University of Washington.

The goal of this session is to introduce the fundamental operations of R, and further to help you do statistical quality control analysis with R.

For the latest slides and developments on bootcamp materials, check out My GitHub

Session module

Each module of the R session will be hold in scheduled Friday morning from 1:00pm to 2:20pm in this spring quarter. Hence please bring your laptop with R and RStudio preinstalled before Friday's lecture.
